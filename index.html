Welcome to Women's Boxing

This is a course designed to teach boxing oriented to self-defense.

Train

Boxing is not just about kicking. It's a very challenging practice. You must train really hard to become a good boxer. Running, jumping, lifting weight, and more is also part of it.

Persevere

Motivation is key. You need to keep going even when you don't see the results. Boxing helps you improve your confidence, developing a strong mind to overcome difficult situations.

Fight

Finally, the fight comes. You have trained hard for months to stay on your foots for 20 minutes in the ring. You may not get to this stage, but you need to be ready in case the fight comes to your life. However, life is a fight, not just a boxing match.
